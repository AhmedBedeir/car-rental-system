Our standard minimum age is 21 years old with a valid license. However:

  • Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young renter fee .
  • Some premium/luxury vehicles require a minimum age of 25 .

We offer three fuel options:

  1. Full-to-Full: Take the car full, return it full (most economical)
  2. Pre-Purchase: Pay for a full tank upfront at local pump rates (no refund for unused fuel)
  3. Convenience Return: Return at any fuel level and pay our per-gallon rate (most expensive)

Yes, you can modify or cancel anytime:

  • No cancellation fees if canceled at least 24 hours before pickup
  • Modifications can be made online or by calling our reservation center
  • Prepaid reservations have different cancellation policies (see your confirmation email)

During peak seasons, special event periods, or for certain vehicle classes, different cancellation policies may apply.

Our grace period is 29 minutes beyond your scheduled return time. After that:

  • 1-2 hours late: 1/2 day charge
  • 2+ hours late: Full day charge
  • 24+ hours late: Additional full day charges plus possible late return fee

Always call us if you anticipate being late - we may be able to adjust your reservation.

If you're involved in an accident:

  1. Ensure everyone's safety and call emergency services if needed
  2. Exchange information with other parties involved
  3. Take photos of the scene, all vehicles, and any damages
  4. File a police report (required for all major accidents)
  5. Contact our roadside assistance immediately at 1-800-RENTAL-HELP
  6. Do not admit fault or sign any documents except for the police report

You'll need to complete an incident report form within 24 hours of the accident.
